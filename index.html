About-Us


What is TED?


TED, which stands for Technology, entertainment and design, is a non-profit organization devoted to spreading ideas. What makes TED different from lectures or speeches is the medium of expression. Usually, a day long, TED features speakers from different arrays of life, outstanding in their fields, who are capable with their experiences and endeavours.


What is TEDx?


X= Independently organized TED event In order to extend the reach of TED’s mission to spread ideas, TEDx was created by TED, which are local, self-organized events. TEDx events, like TED, are held around the world to help bring to the forefront individuals who have contributed positively to the society and whose talks have the power to impact the lives of the listeners.TED and TEDx talks are uploaded to TED.com as well as YOUTUBE, where they are free to be viewed by people around the globe with videos being viewed more than 1.5 million times per day!
